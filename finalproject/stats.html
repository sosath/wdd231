📊 How Standings Work

Teams earn 3 points for a win, 1 for a draw, 0 for a loss. Tiebreakers: Goal Difference → Goals Scored → Head-to-Head.

⚡ Attack & Defense Ratings

Ratings (1–10) are calculated from goals scored/conceded per game, weighted against competition quality. Click a team row to view full ratings.
