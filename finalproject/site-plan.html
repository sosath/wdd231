1. Site Name

EuroStats

Suggested Domain: eurostats-analytics.com

Selection Rationale:

  • Clarity: "Euro" explicitly targets European competitions, especially UEFA Champions League.
  • Professionalism: "Stats" is recognized across the sports industry for analytics and quantitative data.
  • Branding: Short, memorable, and SEO-friendly with relevant keywords.

2. Site Purpose

EuroStats is a dynamic, high-performance football analytics platform for enthusiasts and analysts interested in elite European competitions.

Services & Information Provided:

  • Trending Dashboard: Real-time highlights and current tournament phases.
  • Statistics & Standings: Live league data, top scorers, and team performance metrics via sports APIs.
  • Match Analysis: Head-to-head comparisons, result probabilities, and historical-based forecasts.
  • Personalized Tracking: Follow favorite teams and save analytics using Local Storage.
  • Competition Info: Details on Champions League, Europa League, and major national leagues.

3. User Scenarios

Scenario 1: Casual Fan

Question: "What is Real Madrid's expected lineup and how do they compare to their next opponent?"

A casual fan seeking quick, understandable information about upcoming matches.

Scenario 2: Sports Analyst

Question: "What are the detailed performance metrics for top teams and how do they predict the match outcome?"

A professional analyst requiring in-depth data and statistical comparisons for informed decisions.

Scenario 3: Mobile User

Question: "Can I view standings and upcoming matches of my favorite teams on my phone while traveling?"

A mobile user seeking quick access to updated information with a responsive, intuitive interface.
